Donald Richards








Donald Richards is Professor of Statistics at the Pennsylvania State University.
His research interests include: multivariate statistical analysis, reliability theory,
combinatorics, probability inequalities, representation theory and harmonic analysis,
special functions of matrix argument, astronomy and astrophysics, value investing,
financial derivatives, and applications.
